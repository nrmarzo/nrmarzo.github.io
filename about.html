About Me

Neil Marzo is a fourth-year student at the University of Manitoba, where he is currently working towards a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and specializing in software engineering. He has built mutliple projects along with his peers and classmates for some of his courses. Currently, Neil is expanding his knowledge with web development by taking courses on Udemy and dedicating himself to the #100DaysOfCode challenge. He is able to learn quickly and has strong interpersonal skills. These skills allow him to work confidently as part of a team or independently. When he is not coding, you can find Neil working as a barista at Starbucks or playing his favourite sport, Ultimate frisbee.
